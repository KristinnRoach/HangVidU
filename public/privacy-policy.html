Privacy Policy for HangVidU

Last updated: January 22, 2026

Introduction

HangVidU ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

Data Collection and Usage

1. Authentication Data

We use Google Sign-In for authentication. When you sign in, we receive your email address, name, and profile picture from Google. This information is used solely to identify you within the application and allow other users to find you. Google Firebase handles all authentication data securely according to Google's privacy standards.

2. Peer-to-Peer Communication (WebRTC)

Our application facilitates video and audio calls using WebRTC technology. During a call, video and audio streams are transmitted directly between peers (P2P). We do not record, store, or have access to the content of your video or audio calls.

3. Signaling Data

To establish connections between peers, we use a signaling server (Firebase Realtime Database). Connection metadata (such as IP addresses and ICE candidates) is temporarily processed to route your call but is not permanently stored or used for tracking.

4. User Generated Content

Any text messages or files sent during a session are transmitted directly between users or temporarily stored to facilitate delivery. We do not permanently archive your chat history.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: For hosting, authentication, and database services.
  • YouTube API: If you use the shared video watching feature, your use is subject to the YouTube Terms of Service and Google Privacy Policy.
