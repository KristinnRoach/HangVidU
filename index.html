HangVidU

HangVidU is a messaging app for peer-to-peer video calls, chat, and shared media playback.

If you choose Google import, HangVidU requests read-only Google Contacts access to import your contacts and show which contacts are already using HangVidU.

If you choose Send Email Invite, HangVidU requests Gmail send permission only to send the invite emails you select. HangVidU does not read your inbox.
